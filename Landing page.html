Our Story

Founded in 2010, Petals of Love began as a small flower shop in Mumbai with a passion for bringing traditional Indian floral artistry to modern weddings. Our founder, Priya Sharma, combined her family's generations of floral expertise with contemporary design sensibilities to create something truly special.

Today, we're proud to be one of India's most sought-after wedding florists, having decorated over 500 weddings across the country. Our team of skilled artisans handcraft each arrangement with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring your wedding flowers are as unique as your love story.

We specialize in blending traditional Indian flowers like marigolds and jasmine with exotic blooms to create stunning arrangements that honor your heritage while reflecting your personal style.
